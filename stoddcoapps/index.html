Murderer Among Friends

A round-based party game for 4–16 players. Inspired by Mafia, with interactive ad-lib style storytelling to drive the narrative.

  • 🔀 Players are randomly assigned roles: Murderer, Detective, Doctor, or Guest
  • 🎭 Each role has two unique actions:
    • Murderer: Murder or Recruit
    • Detective: Investigate or Arrest
    • Doctor: Save or Revive
    • Guest: Distract or Wave
  • 📖 Players contribute words to shape the unfolding story
  • 🕵️ Two teams: Innocent (vote out the murderer) vs. Guilty (last players standing)
