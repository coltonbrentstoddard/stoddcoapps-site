Support

If something is not working the way it should, or you just need a hand, we are happy to help.

Contact support

Email: stoddco93@gmail.com

We recommend checking the Help & FAQ first, since many common questions are answered there.

What to include in your message

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your device type and operating system version
  • Your app version, if available
  • Whether the issue involves linking, syncing, reminders, account access, or account deletion
  • Screenshots if they help explain the issue

Response time

We aim to respond within 2 to 5 business days. If your issue involves billing, purchase restoration, or possible data loss, please mention that in the subject line so we can prioritize it appropriately.
